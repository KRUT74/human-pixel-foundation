## Additional Terms for Ethical Use

While this project is licensed under the MIT License, we strongly encourage all users to adhere to the following ethical principles:

### Humanitarian Purpose
This software is intended for humanitarian benefit. Users are encouraged to prioritize human wellbeing in all applications.

### Ethical Constraints
Users should implement appropriate safeguards to prevent:
- Non-consensual use of personal data or imagery
- Generation of harmful, biased, or discriminatory content
- Applications that could cause harm to individuals or groups
- Misuse for surveillance or oppression

### Transparency and Accountability
Users are encouraged to:
- Be transparent about AI-generated content
- Maintain audit trails for accountability
- Engage with affected communities
- Consider global and diverse perspectives

### Research and Development
For research applications:
- Follow established ethical research protocols
- Consider potential dual-use implications
- Engage with ethics review boards
- Publish findings responsibly

While these principles are not legally binding under the MIT License, they reflect the values and intentions of The Human Pixel Foundation. We believe that technology should serve humanity's highest aspirations.
